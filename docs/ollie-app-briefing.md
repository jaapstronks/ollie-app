## Part 3: App Store Screenshots (6 screens)

App Store allows up to 10 screenshots. Aim for 6 strong ones. Each screenshot should have a headline overlay + the app UI underneath.

### Screenshot 1 ‚Äî Hero / First Impression
- **Headline overlay:** "Puppyhood is chaos. Ollie brings the calm."
- **Screen:** Timeline view with a few events logged (morning routine: wake up, potty, breakfast, walk)
- **Vibe:** Clean, warm, inviting. This sells the app.

### Screenshot 2 ‚Äî One-Tap Logging
- **Headline overlay:** "Log anything in seconds"

- **Vibe:** Fast, simple. Show that it's not a complicated form.

### Screenshot 3 ‚Äî Smart Predictions
- **Headline overlay:** "Know what's coming next"
- **Screen:** Stats view showing potty gap prediction, sleep total, outdoor streak
- **Vibe:** Data that actually helps. Not overwhelming charts.

### Screenshot 4 ‚Äî Daily Timeline
- **Headline overlay:** "Your puppy's day, at a glance"
- **Screen:** Full day timeline, nicely filled with various events, showing the visual flow of a day
- **Vibe:** Beautiful, satisfying, "I want to fill this in"

### Screenshot 5 ‚Äî Patterns & Streaks
- **Headline overlay:** "Look how far you've come"
- **Screen:** Weekly overview or streak achievement (e.g. "7 days ‚Äî all potty outside!")
- **Vibe:** Progress, pride. The emotional payoff.

### Screenshot 6 ‚Äî Share with Partner
- **Headline overlay:** "Everyone on the same page"
- **Screen:** Could show sharing/export, or a "today so far" summary that could be shared
- **Vibe:** Teamwork. Practical.

### Design Guidelines for Screenshots
- **Background:** Warm, soft tones (cream/beige or soft gradient ‚Äî not clinical white)
- **Typography:** Bold, friendly sans-serif for headlines (SF Pro Rounded or similar)
- **Phone frame:** Show device frame (iPhone 15 Pro style)
- **Consistency:** Same background treatment across all 6, only headline + screen content changes
- **No stock photos of dogs** ‚Äî let the app UI do the talking. Maybe subtle paw print or bone illustrations as accents.
